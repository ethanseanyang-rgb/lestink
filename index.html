Once Upon A Birthday...

Welcome To Your Quest!

Somewhere up ahead is a little vault, and inside it is a surprise. To get there, you'll climb four stages, each one a different game.

Clear stages 1, 2, and 3 and each one hands you a piece of the vault door. Clear stage 4 and you'll uncover the key. Then it's just a matter of dragging the key into the lock...

You've got 3 hearts to share across the whole climb. Lose a stage and you lose a heart. Run out of hearts and you'll slide back down to the previous stage — hearts refill to 3 so you can keep climbing.

Every stage explains its own rules right before you play, so you'll never be thrown in blind. Good luck — happy birthday! 🎉
